# State DOT Rail Office

A lens, not a reviewer. Captures the state agency that sponsors and subsidizes
corridor service.

## Primary concerns

- Capital match, ongoing operating subsidy, and benefit-cost (DIM-11).
- Political and cross-state feasibility.
- Whether the corridor delivers measurable state benefit.

## Used during

- Benefit-cost and feasibility framing; tier/SLA sponsorship review.
