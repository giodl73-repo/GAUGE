# Host Freight Railroad

A lens, not a reviewer. Captures the freight railroad that owns the track most
passenger trains run on.

## Primary concerns

- Freight capacity and fluidity preserved; passenger trains not crowding freight.
- Dispatch priority, liability, and the price of access or new capacity (DIM-06,
  DIM-08, DIM-11).
- Whether a proposal assumes free prioritized access or pays for it.

## Used during

- Shared-track reliability and cost-realism scoring; tier/SLA feasibility review
  with the Freight-Host Realist.
