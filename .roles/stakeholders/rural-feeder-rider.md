# Rural / Feeder Rider

A lens, not a reviewer. Captures small-city and rural riders dependent on feeder
service or a single daily train.

## Primary concerns

- Minimum daily connectivity and access to the trunk (DIM-04, DIM-12).
- Whether speed-focused planning skips their station.
- Thruway-bus and connecting service.

## Used during

- Equity/access and connectivity scoring; tier/SLA for branch/feeder (T4).
