# Intercity Traveler

A lens, not a reviewer. Captures the traveler choosing between rail, car, and
short-haul air for a 100–500 mile trip.

## Primary concerns

- Door-to-door trip time vs. driving and flying (DIM-02).
- Fare, frequency, and whether a useful train exists when needed (DIM-07).
- Reliability — arriving on time (DIM-06).

## Used during

- Trip-time and frequency scoring; tier/SLA assignment for intercity service.
