# Daily Commuter

A lens, not a reviewer. Captures the regional rider using rail for work travel.

## Primary concerns

- Peak frequency and span of service (DIM-07).
- On-time reliability (DIM-06) — a late train means a missed start.
- Last-mile and transfer to local transit (DIM-04 connectivity).

## Used during

- Frequency/reliability scoring; tier/SLA for regional/commuter tiers (T3).
